# Example 4 — Reasoning Prompt (No Revealed Chain-of-Thought)

Your job is to think through the steps internally and output only the final answer.

Task:
A company spends ₹2,40,000 per month on paid ads.
Conversions: 320 per month.
Average revenue per conversion: ₹3,500.

Calculate:
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- Monthly revenue
- ROI percentage

Important:
- DO NOT reveal intermediate calculations.
- DO NOT show reasoning.
- Only output the final numeric results in a clean table.

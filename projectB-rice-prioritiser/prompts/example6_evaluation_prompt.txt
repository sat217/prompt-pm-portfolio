# Example 6 — Response Evaluation Prompt

Evaluate the AI assistant’s response using the following criteria:

- Accuracy (Does the answer match known facts or correct calculations?)
- Completeness (Are all user requirements addressed?)
- Reasoning Quality (Is the logic sound and internally consistent?)
- Clarity (Is the answer easy to understand and well formatted?)
- Safety (Does the response avoid harmful, misleading, or hallucinated information?)

Your output must include:
1. A score from 1–10
2. A 3–4 sentence evaluation summary
3. A list of specific suggestions for improvement (bullet points)
